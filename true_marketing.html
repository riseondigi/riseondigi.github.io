Our 6-STEP Marketing Formula

Identify

Identifying and dividing your target audience into specific segments based on their age, gender, profession, interests, and behaviors.

Determine

Determining where your target audience spends most of their time.

Define

Defining the message according to the presence of your target audience and the platform they’re on.

Create & Distribute

Creating and distributing content that provides value, educates, entertains, and inspires your target audience, such as blog posts, videos, podcasts, infographics, and social media posts.

Build

Building a strong brand identity that differentiates you from your competitors and establishes trust, credibility, and loyalty with your target audience.

Optimize

Designing and optimizing a series of steps that guide your target audience to from initial awareness to eventual conversion, such as email sequences, landing pages, and retargeting ads.

Hacks To Level-Up Your Marketing Game

1. Don’t Be Sale-sy

Your message to your target-audience should never be sale-sy! It should be attention-grabbing instead.
Remember, your audience’s time is valuable, but their attention is priceless!

2. Powerful Content

Your content should be so powerful that it should make the audience want to know everything about you.
The day you achieve this milestone, sales will become just a by-product.

3. Make Your Content Your USP

All business owners have their USP, but what they don’t know is that their content could be a secret USP too!
Create such distinguished content that sets you apart from your competitors!

4. Know Your Audience

The key to good marketing is knowing your audience both inside and outside your product.
It gives you an edge in succeeding when you know your audience, their pain points, desires, wants, needs, and expectations!

5. Engage Both Current And Potential Audience

There are two types of audiences: one that invests in you and one that doesn’t.
To attract both, you need to tell your current audience how to get the most out of your services, and convince your potential audience that you are worth their time, money, and attention! Your content should cater to both!
