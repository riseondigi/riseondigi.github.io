Hello, I am Shruti Anand , a Marketing Communication Expert For Manufrers & Service Sector SMEs

How did I land to where I am today?

I was a confused kid—not knowing which path I should take, always second-guessing myself, and jumping from being a doctor one day to being an engineer the next. But amidst the chaos and confusion, I ended up taking commerce and wanting to pursue economics.

Did I pursue it? No. Instead, I started my career coaching business owners and began attending lots of practical workshops and training to gain hands-on experience on something that caught my attention— MARKETING!

Here is what I learnt: None of the business owners I worked with took marketing seriously. Of the few that did, their strategies were outdated with no visible results. This realization sparked in me a desire to learn about the power of marketing and delve into its depths.

I believe that in life it’s the unexpected detours that lead us to our true calling. I thought that no matter what happens,

  • I would never do B.Com (Hons), but I did.
  • I would never do sales, but I did (and excelled at it)!
  • I would never run my own business because it’s too much work, but I developed a craze for establishing mine.
  • I would never have a boyfriend, but you know…(just kidding)
In 2021, I founded Rise On Digi, with the belief of MAKING COLLATERALS AS A STRONG FOUNDATION FOR LEAD GENERATION AND CONVERSIONS

Understanding the ABCs of business problems — as well as their solutions — is crucial for businesses of all sizes, but mastering effective marketing strategies can take those same businesses to another level altogether!
