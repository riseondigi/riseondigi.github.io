Frequently Asked Questions about
MARKETING most people are confused about?

How much Investment is enough Investment to get results?

Marketing isn’t planned on the basis of investment. You first need to channelise the exact purpose and audience you want to cater.
Don’t go the traditional way, go the super way!!

What is a fool-proof Digital Marketing strategy?

Honestly, the only fool-proof strategy is when you are able to generate curiosity and attention in the minds of your target group.

Isn’t the role of Marketing to accelerate sales?

No,the role of Marketing is to change the belief systems of your audience so that they see you as an Authority.

Can I run my business without marketing?

Well, you can. But without marketing, your business might only survive, not thrive!

What if my business already has a social presence?

That’s good. But the question is, are you only looking forward to social presence?
Though, social presence is not marketing. It is just like having a letterhead of your company online.

Who are the Biggest Marketers in the world?

Apple, Zomato and BournVita are top on the list as per recent trends.

What should be the intention of Marketing?

The pure intention should be to educate your audience and break their myths they’re believing about the industry/product/trends and other things.
